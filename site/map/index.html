Surface currents

Scrub the shading (speed or ζ/f) through the CMEMS forecast with the time slider at the bottom of the map — 12 h steps from −12 h to +72 h. The flow overlay scrubs with it. The colour scale is in the Currents tab, top-right.

Relative vorticity (ζ/f)

Surface vorticity normalized by the Coriolis parameter, from the same field. Cyclonic (+) and anticyclonic (−) eddies read as opposite-signed lobes. Off by default; toggle it in the Currents tab, where its colour scale also shows.
